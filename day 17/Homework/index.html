Lion | Tiger | Elephant | Giraffe

Lion

The lion is a large cat of the genus Panthera and is known for its majestic mane. Native to Africa, lions are social animals that live in groups called prides.

Tiger

The tiger is the largest species of the cat family and is renowned for its distinctive orange coat with black stripes. Tigers are solitary and are native to Asia.

Elephant

Elephants are the largest land animals on Earth, known for their large ears, trunks, and tusks. They are native to Africa and Asia and live in herds led by a matriarch.

Giraffe

The giraffe is the tallest land animal, famous for its long neck and legs. Native to Africa, giraffes use their height to reach leaves high up in trees.
